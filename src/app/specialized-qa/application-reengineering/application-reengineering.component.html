Application Maintenance

TechnoElevate provides application maintenance by modifying the system to conform to new technical specifications or functional constraints, improving performance, practices, and processes, and providing perpetual maintenance by avoiding potential problems.

Our services include:

  • Re-engineering existing applications to integrate new generation technologies.
  • Fine-tuning processes to improve performance.
  • Forecasting flaws and taking preventive measures to improve availability.
  • Corrective measures to resolve bugs.

Application Management

By elucidating IT management policies, TechnoElevate provides reliability, security, integrity, performance, and availability for your business applications.

Our advanced and dedicated technical support team tends to focus on providing a strong shield for your applications; applying the most recent patches, installing monitoring and reporting tools, and troubleshooting to ensure that systems run smoothly.

Our services include:

  • Firewall and network maintenance.
  • Crucial business modules such as e-commerce and CRM systems.
