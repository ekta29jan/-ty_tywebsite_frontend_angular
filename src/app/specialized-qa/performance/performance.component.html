Do you guys support permanent, C2C, and C2H roles?

TFor all of our clients, we provide C2C and C2H assistance. Permanent solutions are not part of our business strategy.

What conditions must be met for the candidate to be placed on the company payroll if C2H?

If you are looking for C2H there is a lock period of 12 months and later the candidates can be hired with a conversion fee of 8.33 percent added to the offered CTC, which is a standard price in the market. If it is posted for 18 months, however, you can hire the candidates directly to your payroll.

What portion of a commission will you be paying to each candidate?

Since we adhere to a standard rate card, we do not operate on a commission basis.

What major technology stack does Technoelevate support, and how many people are on the waiting list for each technology stack?

We primarily support DevOps, Full Stack, Front End, and Back End Java. For your benefit, I can send you an email with the bench availability, but at any given time we have 60-100 candidates on the bench.

What are the replacement provisions, and what are the alternatives if the candidates don't perform as expected?

Within a week, we will find a replacement if the candidates are underwhelming. Additionally, to ensure that each candidate is functioning effectively, we also offer tech assistance from our end. Furthermore, if there is a substitute, we would like to know 15 days in advance so that we can consider our options.

What is the duration of each candidate's notice period?

If the contract is being terminated, the client must give 30 days' notice of the change. If we are also removing the candidate or if they have submitted their papers, the notice period will also be 30 days.

What agreements do you abide by?

We will share MSA and NDA during the empanelment process, and if a candidate is chosen, we will give them an SOW (statement of work/work order) that details the contract's duration and monthly billing provisions.

Is the rate card inclusive of GST?

Unfortunately, no, GST is excluded from the rate card mentioned. Your business won't be subject to GST if it is located in an SEZ zone.
