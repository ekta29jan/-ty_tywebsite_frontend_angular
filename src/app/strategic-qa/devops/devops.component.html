DEVOPS

Introduction

DevOps, as a culture, is defined as a combination of philosophies, practices, and tools that increases an organization's ability to deliver applications and services at a brisk pace.

Our aim at Test Yantra is to provide:

  • DevOps Testing Solutions that integrate easily with DevOps cycle
  • Increase the frequency & quality of Continuous Deployments
  • Realizing faster time to market
  • Reducing the frequency of release failures

Test Yantra DevOps Capabilities

With constantly changing business needs and emergence of new technologies like Analytics and Cloud, DevOps has made us become more agile and effective in delivering services to business units and their end customers.

We offer solutions that implement DevOps tools thus enabling reliable & faster releases of Applications for the businesses. Our Testing solutions use open source/commercial tools that are connected & can easily integrate in the DevOps cycle.

Test Yantra believes in being abreast with the latest work philosophy, practices & tools and act as a vital cog and provide solutions to our customers in adopting the DevOps culture.
