Test Yantra Test Center of Excellence

Test Yantra houses a centralized Testing Center of Excellence (TCoE) which runs independently advocating best QA practices across projects and teams. Some of the major contirbutions are:

  • Providing Consistency and governance
  • Efficient and structured test processes
  • Current Trends and innovation implementation

With a commitment to provide best possible QA services TCoE ensures in terms of improved quality, lesser time to market and lower cost of ownership.

TEST YANTRA TCOE CAPABILITIES

Test Yantra QA service has developed innovative and critical components that are recommended for a TCoE such as processes, templates, frameworks and tools. These components have given a jump start its TCoE implementation. The key highlights of Test Yantra best practices include:

  • Test Process Management Implementation with Industry best practice
  • Metrics based test management, Gaps and trend analysis
  • Implementation of test accelerators, tools and framework
  • Comprehensive ROI calculation and tracking
  • Delivering continual process improvements
  • Retention of Technical and Domain knowledge
  • Product Quality Dashboards enabling swift decision making

Test Yantra QA service takes complete ownership of End to End testing through the use of methodologies such as Outcome-Based Testing and Service Level Agreements.
