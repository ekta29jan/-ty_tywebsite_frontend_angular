usability testing

INTRODUCTION

"Real goal of Usability testing is to improve the design of application and not just to document its weakness"

At Test Yantra, Usability testing is an important aspect of testing & is given utmost importance.

With Technology advancement, the need to fit in Right features in lesser span of time has motivated us to conduct Usability testing in all projects necessarily.

At Test Yantra usability problems are assessed to the fundamental way people communicate with the Web, and influence from other sites on user behavior.

We gauge application from "what I think" to "what customers think".

PURPOSE

when couple of projects were assessed at Test Yantra on Usability testing being conducted served multiple purposes.

  • Projects exposed to usability testing showed better outcomes
  • Created trust in customers, as we did understand user needs better
  • User needs were best assessed, by identifying those design elements that do or do not work
  • Helped to adapt to continuous changing end user needs
  • Gave an early feedback of product even before it is released
  • Understanding the foundations of human cognition helped to explain and anticipate user behavior
  • Usability tests gave an opportunity to teams a shared learning experience and to establish common ground

USABILITY TESTING PROCESS

Usability testing is conducted in the following steps:

  • Usability test plan
  • Participant Hire
  • Scenario creation
  • Conduct usability test sessions
  • Data Analysis
  • Reporting
  • Usability test recommendations
